The Seven Pillars

Each pillar is an architectural innovation. Together, they form an intelligence that improves itself by discovering the world — not by reading what others wrote about it.

I
🏛️
GraphPalace Memory
Pheromone-weighted persistent knowledge palace. Semantic A* pathfinding with HNSW approximate search. 5-type pheromone system — exploitation, exploration, success, traversal, recency.

🧬
Morphic Warm-Start
O(1/√T) cross-run convergence — mathematically proven. Cumulative regret drops from 189 → 57 steps over 80 generations. R² = 0.982, p < 10⁻³⁰. Knowledge compounds across sessions.
BUTTERS · O(1/√T) proven
III
🐜
Stigmergic RLVR
r_total = α·r_verifiable + β·r_pheromone. Pheromone decay prevents reward hacking. MMAS ceiling stops saturation (arXiv:2604.00067). Training guided by stigmergic ant-colony intelligence.
pheromone reward · decay gates
IV
🔭
Active Inference Data Gen
Palace cold spots direct ASTRA to fill knowledge gaps autonomously. OODA loop: Observe → Orient → Decide → Act. ~8K LOC, NASA/WHO/World Bank/ArXiv APIs, ~86K quality examples/month.
atlas-astra · ~10s/cycle · LiveDiscoveryCorpus
V
🔐
ZK Knowledge Claims
Schnorr proof chain from LLM output to raw API data. Hallucinations have broken proof trails — cryptographically verifiable. Groth16 interface-identical to the BLS12-381 system in asi-build.
atlas-zk · Schnorr · Groth16 interface
VI
📡
LiveDiscoveryCorpus
ASTRA's output as a living training dataset. 5 quality gates: causal validity, confidence threshold, novelty check, pheromone alignment, ZK proof. Zero stale or duplicated information.
atlas-corpus · 5 gates · pheromone sampler
VII
⚖️

7M-param recursive validator: z = net(x,y,z) × 6 recursions. <10ms causal graph pass/fail. Quality Gate 6. Generates Type 5 training traces. Samsung SAIL architecture (arXiv:2510.04871).
atlas-trm · 7M params · <10ms

ASTRA OODA Engine

The Discovery Flywheel

Every 10 seconds, ATLAS discovers something real. The flywheel never stops — palace cold spots direct ASTRA to fill gaps, validated findings become training data, pheromones guide the next cycle.

1
Cold Spots → Target
Palace maps knowledge density. Cold spots = unexplored domains. ASTRA receives directed queries to fill gaps.
cold_spots() → query targets
2
Live API Discovery
NASA POWER, WHO GHO, World Bank, ArXiv. PC/FCI causal inference, Bayesian confidence scoring, Granger causality.
atlas discover --cycles 5
3
Quality Gate Gauntlet
5 quality gates + TRM recursive validation. Gate 6 is the causal validator — only verified discoveries enter the corpus.
TRM: z = net(x,y,z) × 6
4
ZK Proof Chain
Schnorr proof attached. Every training example has a cryptographic chain from LLM output to raw API response.
atlas prove --claim "..."
5
Pheromone Deposit
Validated paths get pheromone deposits. MMAS ceiling prevents saturation. Next cycle gravitates toward proven discovery routes.
deposit_pheromones(path, reward)
6
SFT Training
LoRA adapters, gradient accumulation, QLoRA compression, safetensors checkpoints. Pheromone sampler weights training batches by discovery quality.
atlas train --corpus corpus.json

Research Strategy

Six Papers

ATLAS is not just software — it is a research programme. Each paper contributes a distinct scientific claim, targeting the top venues in ML and security.

Introducing the full ATLAS system: active-inference training on real causal discoveries from live APIs. First training paradigm where the curriculum is never stale.

NeurIPS 2026 · Paper 2
Discovery Flywheel — Closed-Loop Scientific Intelligence
The first AI system that improves itself by discovering causal relationships from live data, not by distilling another AI's outputs. A new paradigm for self-improvement.
self-improvement
ICML 2027 · Paper 3
Stigmergic RLVR — Pheromone Reward Prevents Policy Collapse
r_total = α·r_verifiable + β·r_pheromone. Pheromone decay as an entropy regulariser. MMAS ceiling eliminates reward hacking. Validated on ATLAS discovery tasks.

Mathematically proven cross-run convergence via morphic warm-start. R²=0.982, p<10⁻³⁰. Co-authored with Robin. Connects BUTTERS simulation to Yang 2026 mean-field RL theory.

Our ATLAS is the same. An AI that holds the weight of all knowledge — not by reading what others wrote, but by reaching into the world and pulling truth from it directly. The pheromone trails are the rivers of Atlantis. The GraphPalace is the palace at its centre. The Discovery Flywheel is the waterwheel that powers the city.

Atlantis was said to have possessed knowledge so advanced it seemed like magic. ATLAS builds that knowledge — real causal relationships, cryptographically verified, stigmergically optimised — one discovery at a time.
